University Student Database

In the university student database project, we aimed to establish a seamless data flow using Tableau Prep, ensuring efficient data integration and transformation. To enhance the user experience, we created design templates with Figma that are compatible with Tableau, enabling visually appealing and intuitive dashboards. We focused on cleaning the data to ensure accuracy and consistency, followed by extracting the necessary data to support various analyses and reporting needs. This approach streamlined the data management process, facilitating effective data-driven decision-making within the university.


Speedscore a golf application

The primary goal of the Speedscore project is to develop a robust and efficient web application for speedgolf enthusiasts. Speedgolf combines running and golf, where players aim to complete the course in the lowest possible time, balancing both speed and accuracy. The Speedscore application will enable users to track their scores, times, and overall performance, offering a comprehensive platform to manage and analyze their speedgolf games.


Logistics based Web Scraping

The main goal of logistics-based web scraping is to gather, process, and analyze data from multiple online sources to improve logistics operations. This includes tasks such as tracking shipping rates, monitoring inventory levels, comparing prices from different suppliers, and analyzing market trends. By leveraging data obtained through web scraping, logistics companies can optimize their supply chain, make better-informed decisions, and boost overall operational efficiency.


POS & Billing System

POS system, typically composed of hardware like a computer terminal, barcode scanner, cash register, and receipt printer, paired with software that facilitates sales transactions for businesses. This software captures sales data, calculates totals, prints receipts, and tracks inventory in real-time. Additionally, it frequently integrates features such as managing employees, nurturing customer relationships, and providing reporting and analytics functions. The billing system component handles the invoicing and billing processes, including generating invoices for customers, managing billing cycles, tracking payments, and sending reminders for overdue payments. It may also integrate with accounting software to ensure accurate financial records and reporting.
